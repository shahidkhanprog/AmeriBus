Our Mission

At AmeriBus, we believe that travel should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their zip code or financial situation. We don't just move buses; we move people toward their families, their careers, their education, and their dreams.

Over the last three decades, we have evolved from a small regional carrier serving just three routes in the Northeast to the most expansive and technologically advanced bus network in the United States, serving over 2,500 stations daily across all 50 states, Canada, and Mexico.

Our commitment extends beyond transportation. We're building bridges‚Äîliteral and metaphorical‚Äîthat connect communities, strengthen economies, and reduce our environmental impact. Every journey with AmeriBus is a vote for sustainable, inclusive, and affordable mobility.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

1998

The First Mile

AmeriBus launches its first route between New York City and Philadelphia with just three buses and a vision to make intercity travel affordable for working families. Founder Kamran Khan mortgaged his home to purchase the first three coaches.

2005

Coast to Coast

We officially opened our Los Angeles hub, becoming a truly transcontinental bus service. This expansion connected the East and West coasts through a network of 47 intermediate cities, creating America's first true bus superhighway.

2012

Going International

AmeriBus expands into Canada and Mexico, launching cross-border services and establishing ourselves as North America's premier bus network. We now serve over 200 international destinations.

2018

The Tech Revolution

Introduction of real-time GPS tracking, our award-winning mobile booking app, and contactless boarding systems‚Äîrevolutionizing passenger convenience and setting new industry standards for digital innovation.

2023

Fleet Modernization

Completed the $500M fleet upgrade program, replacing over 1,200 coaches with state-of-the-art buses featuring luxury seating, WiFi, power outlets, and advanced safety systems. Average fleet age now under 3 years.

2026

Net Zero Initiative

AmeriBus commits to transitioning 50% of our fleet to electric and hydrogen propulsion by 2030, with a goal of achieving carbon neutrality by 2035. We're investing $1.2B in sustainable infrastructure.
