Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive at the station?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before departure. Boarding usually begins 15 minutes before the scheduled time. This gives you plenty of time to check in, store your luggage, and find your seat.

What is the baggage policy?

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag (fits in overhead compartment) and one large suitcase stored under the bus (up to 50 lbs) for free. Additional bags can be checked for $20 each. Sports equipment and musical instruments require advance notice.

Can I change my ticket date?

Yes! Tickets can be modified up to 24 hours before departure for a small fee ($10 for Standard, free for Premier) plus any difference in fare. Changes can be made online through our website or mobile app.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available up to 48 hours before departure (minus a $15 processing fee). Within 48 hours, tickets can be exchanged for travel credit valid for one year. Premier tickets include free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure.

Is Wi-Fi available on all buses?

Yes! All AmeriBus coaches feature complimentary Wi-Fi. Standard service provides basic browsing and email, while Premier service offers unlimited high-speed internet for streaming and video calls.
